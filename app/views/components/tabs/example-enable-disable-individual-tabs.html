Tabs Test: Disable/Enable Individual Tabs

The buttons below this paragraph programmatically enable/disable the "form elements" Tab. When a Tab becomes disabled, its corresponding tab panel is checked for any/all IDS Enterprise controls, which are also programmatically enabled/disabled.

This functionality is different from the Enabling/Disabling the Entire Control, which is also possible in the Tabs API.
