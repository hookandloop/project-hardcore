Tabs Test: Close Children on activate()

Related JIRA Tickets: SOHO-4059 and SOHO-4113. The Tabs Control takes advantage of one of the Xi Controls' lifecycle methods, closeChildren(), which will fire whenever a new tab panel is selected. This will close any Xi Controls that have menus, tooltips, popovers, etc. that reside inside of the old tab panel.
